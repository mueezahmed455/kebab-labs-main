🚀 5 Parallel Build Tracks
🛒
Track 1
Core Ordering
  • Homepage (hero, categories, featured, reviews, how-it-works)
  • /menu — sticky sidebar, search, filters, item cards
  • Item modal — variants, quantity, notes, allergens
  • CartDrawer — quantities, min-order warning
  • /order — 4-step checkout
  • Delivery postcode check (postcodes.io)
  • Stripe payment flow (mocked)
  • /track/[id] — realtime status stepper
  • /account — profile, addresses, order history
  • Auth pages — login, register, forgot-pw
  • All API routes from spec
📣
Track 2
Marketing
  • Loyalty points — earn on order, redeem at checkout, account dashboard, tier badges
  • Referral — unique link, credit on signup, share sheet
  • Flash deals — admin creates, countdown timer, auto-banner on menu
  • Social proof — order counter, trending tags, activity toasts
  • Email capture — exit-intent popup, footer form, abandoned cart email
  • Reviews — post-order prompt, stars, photo upload, homepage carousel
✨
Track 3
UX & Interactivity
  • Framer Motion — scroll reveal, 3D card tilt, fly-to-cart, page transitions
  • Hero particle/ember effect
  • Fuzzy search — live filter, highlight matches
  • Dietary + price filters
  • Smart upsells — chip suggestion, meal deal upgrade
  • Min-order progress bar
  • PWA — manifest, service worker, push notifications
  • Combo customizer — visual builder, save, share
  • Favourites — heart icon, account tab, quick reorder
⚙️
Track 4
Operations
  • /kitchen — KDS full-screen, color-coded by age, tap to complete
  • Dynamic wait time — admin sets load level (quiet/normal/busy), shown at checkout + tracking
  • WhatsApp floating button — direct contact
  • WhatsApp/SMS order notifications (Twilio mock)
  • Admin new-order WhatsApp alert
  • Enhanced admin analytics — hourly heatmap, category breakdown, AOV trend, promo performance
🔎
Track 5
SEO & Sharing
  • Restaurant JSON-LD schema
  • Menu item structured data
  • Dynamic OG images (next/og)
  • Sitemap.xml + robots.txt
  • Google My Business deep-link
  • Social share sheet on items + cart
  • Shareable combo links with OG preview
  • "I just ordered!" post template
